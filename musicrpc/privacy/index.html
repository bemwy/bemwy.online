Privacy Policy

MusicRPC  |  Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how bemwy ("Developer," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the MusicRPC application ("Application"). By using the Application, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

The Application collects the minimum information necessary to provide its functionality:

  • Now Playing Data: The title, artist, album name, and playback position of the track currently playing in Apple Music on your device. This information is read locally from your device and transmitted to Discord's API solely to display your Rich Presence activity.
  • Discord OAuth Token: An access token and refresh token issued by Discord upon your authorization. These tokens are stored locally on your device in the iOS Keychain and are used exclusively to authenticate requests to Discord's API on your behalf.
  • Last.fm Session Key (optional): If you choose to connect a Last.fm account, a session key issued by Last.fm upon your authentication is stored locally in the iOS Keychain. Your Last.fm username and password are transmitted directly and exclusively to Last.fm's API over HTTPS and are never stored by the Application beyond the session key returned by Last.fm.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.);
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data;
  • Analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or usage statistics;
  • Payment or financial information of any kind;
  • Any data beyond what is described in Section 1.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected by the Application is used solely to provide the core functionality of the Application — specifically, displaying your currently playing Apple Music track as a Discord Rich Presence activity and, optionally, scrobbling tracks to Last.fm. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

All authentication tokens (Discord OAuth token and Last.fm session key) are stored exclusively on your device using the iOS Keychain with the kSecAttrAccessibleAfterFirstUnlock protection class, which means they are encrypted and accessible only after your device has been unlocked at least once following a restart. No data is transmitted to or stored on any server controlled by the Developer.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your information to any third party. Data is transmitted only to the following third-party services as necessary to operate the Application:

  • Discord Inc. — Now playing information is sent to Discord's API to display Rich Presence. Your use of Discord is governed by Discord's Privacy Policy at discord.com/privacy.
  • Last.fm / CBS Interactive Inc. (optional) — If you enable Last.fm integration, now playing and scrobble data is sent to Last.fm's API. Your use of Last.fm is governed by Last.fm's Privacy Policy at last.fm/legal/privacy.
  • Apple Inc. — Album artwork URLs are retrieved from the iTunes Search API. No personally identifiable information is transmitted in these requests.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with applicable law or a valid legal process.

6. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You may:

  • Revoke Discord authorization at any time via Discord's Authorized Apps settings at discord.com/settings/authorized-apps;
  • Disconnect your Last.fm account from within the Application's settings;
  • Delete all stored data by uninstalling the Application, which removes all Keychain entries associated with the Application.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Your continued use of the Application after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Developer on Discord at @bemwy.
